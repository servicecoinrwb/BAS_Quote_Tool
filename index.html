1. The Golden Rule: One Wire = One Point

Do not estimate based on "functions" (e.g. "Heating"). Estimate based on wires connected to the controller.

2. Example: Standard Rooftop Unit (RTU)

Use this to estimate points for a typical commercial packaged unit.

Device / Function Point Type Point Count Notes
Fan Start/Stop DO 1 Relay command to start fan
Fan Status DI 1 Current switch (proof of flow)
Cooling Stage 1 DO 1 Compressor 1
Cooling Stage 2 DO 1 Compressor 2
Heating Stage 1 DO 1 Gas valve / Strip heat 1
Heating Stage 2 DO 1 Gas valve / Strip heat 2
Economizer Damper AO 1 0-10V Signal to open/close
Supply Air Temp AI 1 Duct Sensor
Return Air Temp AI 1 Duct Sensor
Zone Temp (T-Stat) AI 1 Wall Sensor
TOTAL 10 Points Enter "10" in the calculator

3. Example: VAV Box (Variable Air Volume)

Device / Function Point Type Count Notes
Damper Actuator AO 1 Opens/closes air flow
Zone Temp AI 1 Wall Sensor
Discharge Air Temp AI 1 Strip heat check
Reheat Valve (or Stage 1) AO / DO 1 Hot water or Electric
TOTAL 4 Points

4. Point Types Legend

  • DI (Digital Input): Watching a switch (On/Off). Ex: Filter Alarm, Fan Status.
  • DO (Digital Output): Turning a relay on (On/Off). Ex: Fan Start, Heat Stage 1.
  • AI (Analog Input): Reading a changing value. Ex: Temperature, Humidity, Pressure.
  • AO (Analog Output): Sending a variable signal (0-100%). Ex: Damper position, Speed drive.
