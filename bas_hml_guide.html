Technical Strategy: The JACE Swap

Phase 1: Physical Migration

  • 1. The Audit Identify the "Trunk" wires connected to the JACE RS-485 ports. Note the protocol (usually BACnet MS/TP or N2) and baud rate (38400 or 76800).
  • 2. Demolition Power down the JACE. Unplug the green MSTP connectors. Remove the JACE from the DIN rail. Note: We leave the field controllers (VAVs, Boilers) untouched. We are only replacing the "Brain".
  • 3. Connection Mount the Cypher Edge-X1. Land the existing RS-485 trunk wires onto Cypher Port 1 & 2. Power up with 24VAC.

Phase 2: Digital Takeover

  • 4. Discovery Scan Log into Cypher OS. Set Port 1 to BACnet MS/TP. Run "Discover". The system will ping every device on the wire and populate the database automatically.
  • 5. Point Mapping Since field devices are standard BACnet, Cypher pulls in their points (Room Temp, Setpoint) instantly. No new programming required at the VAV level.
  • 6. Graphics Upgrade The old JACE graphics (Java) are gone. We build modern HTML5 dashboards in Cypher that point to the newly discovered data tags.
